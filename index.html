Project Synopsis

Despite many jobs and education sites, the Skills Gap is growing. We want to create easily accessible, user-friendly software to match skills to jobs better. The original project was to align employers with educators to provide custom education roadmaps for job candidates based on their current abilities and missing skills needed to apply to greater job opportunities. The first group sponsored at RIT focused on developing a skills training and talent acquisition platform to give candidates a more transparent educational roadmap to jobs they want and contact with the employers that align with the skills being acquired. After more research, we pivoted with our second and third teams to focus on fast candidate-toemployee connections with a more dating app format.
